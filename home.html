The Journey

Having first met at their auditions for university, Michelle Harris and Alex Dopierala continued to work together during their Music degree. In their second year of study, DELIAH was born.

Taking influence from contrasting artists such Amy Winehouse and Kendrick Lamar, the duo have a self confessed eclectic taste in music. This, paired with their organic and collaborative writing style, are the ingredients for their unique sound which Michelle describes as “A collision of two personalities”. The combination of Michelle’s soulful voice and Dop’s intricate guitar playing bring a certain class and retro vibe to an undeniable contemporary groove.

"It’s effortlessly cool, and has a kind of seductive sophistication about it..." - Fresh Beats

From playing along side the likes of Anthony David at the prestigious Jazz Café in Camden as an acoustic duo, to playing big stages such as the Liverpool International Music Festival with a 5 piece band; DELIAH have been taking their versatile, energetic live set around the UK.

"Harris poses a considerable vocal ability that can add another smooth, sleek layer to the mix" - Getintothis

DELIAH has gained recognition and airplay from the likes of the BBC and EmpireRadio Magazine, whilst the launch for the latest track, 'Spying On Life', was a sell-out, D.I.Y event earning them the title of “Liverpool’s Hidden Gem” – The Revu.
